Some things I like.

In 1675 Isaac Newton wrote, “If I have seen further it is by standing on the shoulders of Giants.” I have certainly made weighing down the shoulders of mythical beings a hobby of mine, drawing inspiration from all sorts of things, and this is simply a collection of them that I’m quite fond of or particularly admire.

But don’t be mistaken, this is not an exhaustive list of the things I enjoy. I am currently listening to Ken Carson’s Freestyle 2 while writing this, however, Ken’s work along with many others were not included below. It is an amazing tune, but not exactly one to make me feel all sentimental and introspective, let alone brim with my own verses.
