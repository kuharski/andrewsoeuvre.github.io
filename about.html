A little about myself.

My intention here is not to bore you, as I could only hope you’re chomping at the bit to dive into the following pages, but I feel a small introduction can go a long way. So, I present to you, my ‘About’ page. I would have someone else write a listless biography to include here, but I don’t quite garner that level of attention yet, and certainly don’t fall into the swashbuckling, poetic protégé trope; oh, a man can only dream. For now, I am simply a student at the University of Waterloo who likes to jot down ideas and rhymes while doodling in the margins before bedtime.

One summer night, I was flipping through my notebook trying to remember which muscle group I was going attack at the gym the morning after, when I realized I had accumulated quite the collection of poems and short stories. This collection of mine for years now has solely laid in the confines of my bedside table. However, I thought, what fun it might be to digitize it. To free it.

I’m currently majoring in computer science, and potentially a bit too comfortable making my way around a text editor (VS Code I sing your praises). So, I thought why not leverage this to build my own website for my writing, giving it a new home to live on, and I made sure to let my own drawings and photos tag along. I tinkered with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for a few weeks and voila, here it is! Certainly, a lot less dreary than a bedside drawer, don’t you think? Oh, I know I’ve taken up an awful lot of your time already, so scurry on then and check the rest out!
